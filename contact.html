Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry? +

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent support requests, we recommend using our live chat feature available in the mobile app.

Where can I get technical support for the app? +

Technical support is available through multiple channels: in-app chat support, email at support@touristguideapp.com, or by phone during our operating hours. For the fastest resolution, please include details about your device model and operating system version.

How can I report an issue with a location or attraction information? +

We appreciate your help in keeping our information accurate! You can report inaccuracies directly through the app by selecting the attraction and using the "Report Issue" feature, or by emailing content@touristguideapp.com with the specific details.

Do you offer partnership opportunities for local businesses? +

Yes, we collaborate with local businesses, tour operators, and accommodation providers. For partnership inquiries, please email partnerships@touristguideapp.com with details about your business and proposed collaboration.
